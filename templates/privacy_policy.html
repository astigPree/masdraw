Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Introduction

MasDraw ("we", "us", "our") operates the website https://masdraw.masbate.top. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our anonymous drawing platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping MasDraw as transparent as possible.

Information We Collect

MasDraw is designed to be anonymous. We do not require accounts, email addresses, or personal identification. Here is what we collect:

  • Drawing data: The artwork you create on the canvas, including strokes, shapes, text, and a preview image, is stored on our server so you can save and return to your work.
  • Recovery keys: A hashed version of your recovery key is stored so you can recover your drawing later. We do not store the plain-text recovery key after it is first shown to you.
  • Browser cookie: A browser token cookie is used to associate your browser session with your most recent drawing for convenience. This cookie does not identify you personally.
  • IP address: We use hashed IP addresses for rate-limiting purposes to prevent abuse. We do not store raw IP addresses or link them to your drawings.
  • Server logs: Standard web server logs may include IP addresses, browser type, and request timestamps. These are used for security and debugging only.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Store and serve your drawings when you access them
  • Provide the recovery key system for anonymous drawing access
  • Prevent abuse through rate limiting
  • Display published drawings in the public showcase
  • Maintain and improve the platform

Publishing Drawings

Drawings are private by default. When you choose to publish a drawing, it becomes publicly visible in the MasDraw showcase. Published drawings include the artwork preview image and the title you provide. No personal information is attached to published drawings.

Data Storage and Security

Drawing data is stored on our server. Recovery keys are stored in hashed form for security. We take reasonable measures to protect stored data, but no system is completely secure. Since MasDraw is anonymous, the risk to personal privacy is minimal.

Cookies

MasDraw uses a single functional cookie (browser token) to identify your most recent drawing session for convenience. This cookie does not track you across websites and contains no personal information. You can clear this cookie at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

MasDraw may use third-party services for hosting, content delivery, and security (such as Cloudflare). These services may collect their own data according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

MasDraw does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the platform is anonymous and does not require registration, minimal data is collected from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of MasDraw after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through the website at https://masdraw.masbate.top.
