General questions

About MasDraw and how it works

What is MasDraw?

MasDraw is a free anonymous online drawing platform for people in Masbate. It allows users to create digital drawings without creating an account, save privately using a recovery key, and optionally share selected drawings in a public showcase.

Do I need to create an account?

No. MasDraw is fully anonymous. You can start drawing immediately without signup, email, or login. A recovery key is provided so you can return to your drawing later.

Is MasDraw free to use?

Yes. MasDraw is completely free. There are no paid features, subscriptions, or hidden costs.

Saving and recovery

How saving and recovery keys work

How do I save my drawing?

Click the Save button on the drawing canvas. Your drawing is saved on the server. Keep your recovery key safe - it is the only way to access your drawing again from a different browser or device.

What is a recovery key?

A recovery key is a private code generated when you start a new drawing. It works like a password. Paste it on the Recover Drawing page to reopen your artwork. MasDraw does not store your identity, so the key is the only way to access your work.

What happens if I lose my recovery key?

If you lose your recovery key, there is no way to recover your drawing. MasDraw does not collect email or account information, so the key is your only access method. Always save it in a safe place.

Publishing and sharing

How to publish and share your drawings

Can I publish my drawing publicly?

Yes. After saving your drawing and adding a title, you can publish it to the MasDraw public showcase. Published drawings are visible to all visitors. You control when and whether your work becomes public.

Can I delete a published drawing?

Currently, once a drawing is published, it cannot be unpublished through the interface. Contact MasDraw support if you need a published drawing removed.

What devices work with MasDraw?

MasDraw works in any modern web browser on desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile phone. No app download is required.
