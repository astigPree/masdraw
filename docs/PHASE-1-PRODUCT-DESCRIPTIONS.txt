PHASE 1 PRODUCT DESCRIPTIONS
Project: MasDraw
Primary domain: https://masdraw.masbate.top
Current product stage: MVP with SEO discovery, anonymous drawing, private recovery, and public showcase.

1. PRODUCT SUMMARY

MasDraw is a free anonymous online drawing platform focused on Masbate-inspired creativity. Users can open a browser-based canvas without signup, create a drawing, save it privately with a recovery key, and publish selected finished artwork to a public showcase.

The product is not trying to compete with professional design tools. Its practical wedge is simple: "Can I draw anonymously in Masbate online?" MasDraw should own that search intent and convert visitors into creators.

2. REAL PROBLEM

People who want to make simple digital drawings usually face friction:
- account creation
- app installation
- social profile exposure
- complex design tools
- no simple local creative space for Masbate-related artwork

MasDraw solves this by giving users a fast creative workflow:
- open the site
- start drawing
- save with a recovery key
- publish only if they choose

The local Masbate angle is the positioning advantage. It makes the product more searchable and more memorable than a generic drawing website.

3. TARGET USERS

Primary users:
- students who need a fast drawing tool for simple ideas or school activities
- local Masbate artists and hobbyists
- people who want to create anonymous sketches without a public profile
- visitors searching for Masbate drawing, anonymous drawing, or local creative pages

Secondary users:
- teachers who want a simple no-signup creative activity
- community groups that want lightweight visual submissions
- local event organizers who may want public art prompts or contests

Not the target user in Phase 1:
- professional illustrators needing layers, exports, tablets, asset libraries, or collaboration
- design teams needing Figma-style workflows
- users needing private encrypted storage with account-level permissions

4. BEST STRATEGY

Phase 1 strategy is SEO-led product validation.

The product should rank for narrow, local, high-intent queries before trying to become a broad drawing platform. The strongest keywords are:
- anonymous drawing in Masbate
- Masbate drawing online
- draw anonymously in Masbate
- MasDraw
- Mas Draw
- Masbate online sketch
- Masbate art showcase

The best product loop:
1. User discovers MasDraw from search or social share.
2. User opens the canvas without signup.
3. User saves a drawing and keeps the recovery key.
4. User publishes a titled drawing.
5. Published drawing becomes a new public page.
6. Public page expands search coverage and sends more users back to the canvas.

The current architecture already supports this loop.

5. CORE FEATURES

Current Phase 1 features:
- anonymous drawing session creation
- recovery key generation
- recovery key hashing
- browser token cookie for recent drawing convenience
- drawing canvas with brush, eraser, highlighter, text, line, rectangle, circle, fill, color picker, undo, symmetry, zoom, pan, and fullscreen
- canvas size options for landscape, square, portrait, and wide
- server-side save endpoint for drawing data and preview image
- publish endpoint requiring saved preview and non-empty title
- public showcase with featured and latest drawings
- individual published artwork pages
- quick preview modal in showcase
- SEO landing page for "Anonymous Drawing in Masbate Online"
- FAQ, About, Privacy Policy, Terms, sitemap, robots.txt, favicon, and structured data

6. USER VALUE PROPOSITION

Short version:
Draw anonymously in Masbate online. No signup. Save privately. Publish when ready.

Long version:
MasDraw gives people in Masbate a simple browser canvas for local creative expression. Users do not need an account or app download. They can sketch privately, recover their work with a key, and publish finished drawings to a public Masbate showcase.

7. POSITIONING

Best positioning:
MasDraw is the anonymous drawing website for Masbate-inspired online sketches.

Avoid positioning it as:
- a professional drawing suite
- a social network
- a private vault
- an AI art generator
- a general Canva/Figma alternative

The product should stay focused on speed, anonymity, local identity, and public discovery.

8. PHASE 1 EXECUTION STEPS

Immediate product priorities:
1. Keep one canonical domain: https://masdraw.masbate.top.
2. Make sure old domains redirect permanently to the canonical domain.
3. Strengthen the landing page answer for exact search queries like "Can I draw anonymously in Masbate?"
4. Add more real published drawings with descriptive titles.
5. Add local backlinks from Masbate-related pages, posts, directories, or community groups.
6. Keep the canvas stable on mobile, because casual users may come from phones.
7. Add an admin/manual removal process for published drawings.
8. Track basic metrics: visits, started drawings, saved drawings, published drawings, recoveries, and showcase clicks.

9. MONETIZATION

Do not monetize too early. Phase 1 should validate usage and search demand first.

Best future monetization paths:
- sponsored local art prompts from businesses or events
- paid featured placement for contests or community campaigns
- school or organization private gallery package
- downloadable certificate or showcase badge for events
- local creative contest pages
- lightweight ads only after stable organic traffic exists

Avoid:
- charging individual casual users immediately
- forcing accounts before validating anonymous usage
- adding heavy marketplace features before there is supply and demand

10. SCALING PLAN

Phase 1:
- SQLite is acceptable for validation.
- Base64 preview images in the database are acceptable for low traffic.
- Manual moderation is acceptable.

Phase 2:
- move preview images to object storage
- move database to PostgreSQL
- add moderation queue
- add delete/unpublish request flow
- add analytics events
- improve recovery performance
- add image export/download
- add prompt-based drawing challenges

Phase 3:
- organization/event galleries
- contest mode
- user opt-in profiles
- local creative campaigns
- paid sponsorship packages

11. SUCCESS METRICS

Phase 1 metrics:
- Google indexed canonical pages
- Bing indexed canonical pages
- favicon and brand display correctly in search
- top 10 search position for "anonymous drawing in Masbate"
- at least 100 organic visits
- at least 20 started drawings
- at least 5 saved drawings
- at least 3 published drawings with real titles
- at least 2 local backlinks or mentions

Product quality metrics:
- save success rate
- publish success rate
- recovery success rate
- mobile canvas usability
- average number of strokes/elements per saved drawing

12. BRUTAL FEEDBACK

MasDraw has a good narrow wedge, but it is fragile if it stays as only a drawing toy. The SEO angle gives it distribution, but the product needs real published artwork and local backlinks to become trusted by Google. Empty or low-quality showcase content will weaken the product.

The biggest risk is not technology. The biggest risk is lack of usage. A drawing platform needs visible activity. If users arrive and see no strong examples, they may not publish. Seed the showcase with real Masbate-inspired drawings and clear titles.

The second biggest risk is trust. Anonymous tools can attract low-quality or abusive content. Phase 1 needs at least a manual moderation and removal workflow, even if it is admin-only.

The third risk is overbuilding. Do not add accounts, subscriptions, AI tools, social feeds, comments, likes, or complex editing until the core loop proves demand.

The correct Phase 1 goal is simple:
Own the local anonymous drawing search intent, make the canvas usable, publish enough real artwork to look alive, and prove people want this before scaling.
